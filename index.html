Welcome to DRUM SIM

DrumSim is a ‘virtual’ drum set that allows you to learn some basic drumming with the help of your keyboard/mouse/touch.
Play along with songs and see in real time what drum you’re hitting that’s making the sounds you hear that comprise a beat.

HOW TO PLAY THE DRUMSIM

LEGEND:

For Kick/Bass Drum: 'b' on keyboard

For Snare: 's' on keyboard

For hi-hat: 'h' on keyboard
